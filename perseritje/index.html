Owls

Owls can hear sounds that humans can’t. Their ears are placed asymmetrically (one higher than the other), which helps them figure out exactly where a sound is coming from, even in total darkness. Some owls can hear a mouse moving under snow or leaves!

Owls have huge eyes that let in lots of light — perfect for seeing in the dark. Their eyes are so big that they can’t move them around like humans do, which is why they have to turn their heads instead.

The edges of their feathers are shaped to reduce noise when they fly. This makes them almost completely silent, so their prey doesn’t hear them coming.
